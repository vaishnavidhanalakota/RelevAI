Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload my resume?

Go to the My Resumes section and click the upload button. Supported formats include PDF, DOC, and DOCX files up to 5MB.

How are job matches calculated?

Our algorithm analyzes your resume skills, experience, and preferences to match you with relevant job opportunities.

Can I have multiple resumes?

Yes, you can upload and manage multiple resumes tailored for different job types or industries.

How do I improve my resume score?

Use our resume analysis tools to identify areas for improvement and follow our optimization suggestions.
