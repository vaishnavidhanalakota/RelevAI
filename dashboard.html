Help Center

How do I upload a resume?

Click “Upload New Resume” in the Resume Analysis section. We accept PDF and DOCX files.

What is the relevance score?

It’s a 0–100% match between your resume and the job description, based on skills, experience, and keywords.

How are suggestions generated?

Our AI analyzes missing skills and suggests improvements to better match your target jobs.

Why is my score low?

You may be missing key skills or keywords from the job description. Check the “Missing Skills” section and use “Improve Resume” for tips.
